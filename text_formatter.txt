<li>Tomato Bisque Soup</li>
<li>¼ lb. Butter</li>
<li>½ medium onion, finely chopped</li>
<li>¾ cup flour</li>
<li>1 quart whole milk, warmed</li>
<li>1 46oz. Can tomato juice</li>
<li>2- 8oz. Cans tomatoes, peeled and diced</li>
<li>¾ tsp. Ground Greek Oregano</li>
<li>dash of white pepper</li>
<li>¼ cup sugar (optional)</li>s
<li>¾ tsp. Kosher salt</li>

<li>On medium heat, sauté onions in butter until transparent and softened.</li>
<li>Sift in the flour, stirring vigorously and constantly – at least 1 minute.</li>
<li>Whisk in warmed milk until it starts to thicken, about 10 minutes or until it starts to bubble.</li>
<li>Add the tomato juice, Worcestershire sauce and diced tomatoes, stirring constantly just to a boil.</li>
<li>Slowly add the rest of the ingredients and bring back to boil.</li>

Yield: Approx.  1 gallon
Note:  This recipe can be stored in the freezer up to 6 months.
